The Power of Chainn

Efficient connections

No in-between links, direct transport to
the customer and therefore a super
fresh product. That is Chainn. With our
dedicated app, you have real-time insight
into the entire food chain.

Strong product focus

The right product, from the right area at
the right time: that is our specialty. We
work with growers from all over the
world, enabling us to deliver the best
quality all year round.

The best quality

We strive for the highest achievable
quality. We therefore choose our
growers carefully and aim for open
cooperation and fair trade.

Knowledge and know-how

We act on years of experience, passion
for the business and in-depth knowledge
of the market and products. That is what
makes us a market leader in citrus and
specialist in vegetables and melons.

Our assortment

We offer an extensive range of citrus, vegetables and
melons. We only go for the best quality, with the best people
and work with growers from Morocco, Spain, Israel, Egypt,
Argentina, Peru and South Africa.
